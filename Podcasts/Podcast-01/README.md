# Podcast Title
This is Not Just a Podcast. This is a Moo-vement.

**Episode:** Podcast 01

## Short Context
This episode exists to answer one simple but important question:

**What is Winking Cow, really?**

Before the stickers, before the tweets, before the timelines and the winks, there was intent. This opening episode sets the emotional and philosophical foundation of the Winking Cow project. It explains why this podcast exists, why it is voice-only, and why the project chooses meaning over noise.

This is not an announcement.  
It is a declaration of tone, soul, and direction.
